About Me

I'm a graphic designer turned front-end developer turned interaction designer. I'm passionate about well designed interfaces (and just about anything).

I grew up in Houston, Texas and moved to Utah when I was 18 to attend Brigham Young University. I studied PR for one semester when I realized that I enjoyed creating the graphics for marketing for than the writing. I picked up a job doing web design and have loved it ever since. I will recieve my BA in graphic design in spring of 2015.

I lived for two years in Costa Rica from ages 19 to 21 on a church mission. I speak fluent Spanish. I've been happily married since summer of 2014.

Once I graduate I'm looking to dig deep into user experience design. I've had some great mentors that have shown me the impact that good UX can have. I'm a hard worker and a team player. Having a background in both design and development, I communicate well to designers and engineers. If you're an employer, I hope you'll consider the impact I could have on your company.
